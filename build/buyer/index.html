How does intellectual property work
This platform takes NO responsibility in protecting intellectual property. But there are things you can do to protect your IP. For example, creating seperate projects so not one single company has all the information about your project. In this case you can create two projects for each different part and invite only relevant companies by manufacturing process speciality. One other way is to use our sourcing services. We also use the same methods to ensure not all data is in a single place accessed by 1 individual or company
